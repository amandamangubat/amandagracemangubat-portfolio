About Us

Pawlax is a premium pet grooming center.

Professional Pet Grooming Our professional and experienced pet groomers provide premium grooming services with utmost care to ensure that your furbabies get the best pet grooming experience in the Metro’s posh and cozy pet salon.

Health & Wellness To ensure that your furbabies are in top shape, Paws & Friend’s (our subsidiary brand) in-house veterinarians can provide consultations and preventive pet health care & wellness services. Strictly by appointment only. Premium Pet Hotel You can now travel and do your daily errands without worrying about your furbabies, Paws & Fur’s convenient and comfortable pet hotels got your back. Your dogs and cats can have their daycare, pet boarding, or staycation while you enjoy your worry-free travels.
