Passion for Visual Storytelling

Halley Carvajal is a driven college student pursuing a degree in Bachelor of Multimedia Arts. With a keen eye for design and a deep love for visual storytelling, she enjoys crafting compelling content that blends creativity and purpose.

A Creative Mindset

From digital illustrations to short video productions, Halley explores multiple mediums to express her ideas. Her academic projects often reflect her interest in social issues and advocacy, aiming to inspire positive change through design.

Active on Campus

Beyond the classroom, Halley is involved in her university’s multimedia organization, where she collaborates on student-led initiatives and workshops. She also volunteers as a media editor for campus events and publications.

Future Aspirations

Halley aims to become a creative director or motion designer post-graduation. She’s constantly honing her skills in animation, UI/UX design, and video editing—believing that learning never stops in the world of media arts.
