About CIIT

College of Arts and Technology

Welcome to CIIT College of Arts and Technology! We’re a passionate community of artists, innovators, and technologists. Everything you see on this website was created by our talented students, faculty, and staff—CIITzens united by creativity.

If you're an aspiring digital artist, filmmaker, game developer, or programmer, you're in the right place. Your journey can begin here, and who knows—your work might be featured on our website someday!

Located in Quezon City, CIIT is a premier institution offering programs in Multimedia Arts, Visual Arts, Programming, and Game Development. Get to know us more through our official website.
