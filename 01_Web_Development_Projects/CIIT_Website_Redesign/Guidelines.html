Student Showcase Guidelines

General

I. For your work to be approved for the screening, please be guided by the following:

  • The output can be in any form ranging from Finished works to Work in progress (WIPs), Illustrations, Animation, Animatic, Game, 3D, Poster, Graphic design, etc.
  • The submission form must be completed.
  • The student must submit a signed digital copy of the Student’s Work's consent form. If there are any concerns regarding the consent form, please let us know. (studentshowcase@ciit.edu.ph)
  • The output should be no more than 1GB and above.
  • The output must not be in any form of malicious intent.
  • To protect your work, the output can have a signature and/or watermark unless it is intended.
  • The student must submit an online shareable folder (e.g. Google Drive) that has all the asked requirements. *Further expanded in In Submitting Works*.

II. Output works will be approved or declined within 2-3 days or within the week of your submission. In case of technical issues in the system, the website will automatically close down for a while and it will not be accessible to anyone as of the moment.

III. Outputs that do not meet the requirements or follow the guidelines listed above or that violate CIIT policies on safe spaces and inclusivity (e.g. harassment) will be removed by authorized school personnel and may result in further disciplinary sanctions (Student Handbook revised 2021 under Minor Offense #8 & #13).

In Submitting Works

I. In submitting works, only one (1) work can be submitted per filled form. To submit another work, the student must fill and submit another form.

II. A maximum of one (1) online shareable folder (e.g. Google Drive) with given general access to the Student Showcase’s screeners can be submitted per filled form.

III. The folder name should be as followed LASTNAME_FIRSTNAME_TITLEOFWORK

IV. For your work to be showcased, the following requirements must be accomplished and contained in the student’s shareable online folder:

  • A PDF copy of a written document containing the information of the work: Name/Title of Work, a Description of the Work (500 maximum word limit), and state what kind of work it is (e.g. Animation, Character Design, Mobile UI/UX, Short Film, Skit, etc.)
  • The student’s created work/output.
  • Documentation of the process of creating the work.
  • A signed PDF copy of the Student’s Work’s consent form.

Failure to accomplish the requirements will lead to the submitted form being filed and/or labeled as incomplete and not eligible for the showcase.

V. For works that are in Audio and/or Video format (e.g. GIFs, MP4s, MP3, etc.), it must either be in a ZIP file format and/or most preferably a link that redirects to the work (e.g. Youtube link).

VI. A maximum of two (2) forms can be submitted by a student per month. This limitation is set so that the Student Showcase’s screeners have ample time to process the submitted forms. This limitation is also set so that every student’s works are accounted for and have ample time to be showcased.

VII. For submitted forms where the files are corrupted due to technical issues, the student will be notified via email about their submitted work. In the case of corrupted files, the student can resubmit another form.
